# 🧠 Learning Notes: The 37 Seminal AI Papers

**Source:** LinkedIn list compiling the most critical research papers for AI Engineers.

## 📌 The Core Thesis
Most AI engineers focus on fine-tuning models or writing prompts. The top 1% understand the underlying architectural shifts by reading the foundational papers. This curated list tracks the evolution from the invention of the Transformer to modern Agentic Alignment.

---

## 📚 The Reading List

### 🔸 2017 - 2019: The Foundations (The Transformer Revolution Begins)
1. **Attention Is All You Need:** The invention of the Transformer architecture.
2. **BERT:** Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers.
3. **GPT-1:** Improving Language Understanding by Generative Pre-Training.
4. **GPT-2:** Language Models are Unsupervised Multitask Learners.
5. **XLNet:** Generalized Autoregressive Pretraining for Language Understanding.
6. **RoBERTa:** A Robustly Optimized BERT Pretraining Approach.
7. **T5:** Exploring the Limits of Transfer Learning with a Unified Text-to-Text Transformer.
8. **Megatron-LM:** Training Multi-Billion Parameter Language Models Using Model Parallelism.

### 🔸 2020 - 2023: Scaling Up (Bigger Models, Better Performance)
9. **GPT-3:** Language Models are Few-Shot Learners.
10. **Scaling Laws:** Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models.
11. **ELECTRA:** Pre-training Text Encoders as Discriminators Rather Than Generators.
12. **ZeRO:** Memory Optimizations Toward Training Trillion Parameter Models.
13. **Switch Transformers:** Scaling to Trillion Parameter Models with Simple and Efficient Sparsity.
14. **LoRA:** Low-Rank Adaptation of Large Language Models.
15. **PaLM:** Scaling Language Modeling with Pathways.
16. **Chinchilla:** Training Compute-Optimal Large Language Models.
17. **InstructGPT / RLHF:** Training language models to follow instructions with human feedback.
18. **Chain-of-Thought:** Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning in Large Language Models.
19. **FlashAttention:** Fast and Memory-Efficient Exact Attention with IO-Awareness.
20. **Constitutional AI:** Harmlessness from AI Feedback.
21. **LLaMA:** Open and Efficient Foundation Language Models.
22. **GPT-4:** Technical Report.
23. **Llama 2:** Open Foundation and Fine-Tuned Chat Models.
24. **Mamba:** Linear-Time Sequence Modeling with Selective State Spaces.

### 🔸 2024 - Present: Alignment & Agents (The Age of Intelligence and Control)
25. **Mixtral of Experts:** A Sparse Mixture-of-Experts Language Model.
26. **Concept Cones:** Analyzing representation spaces.
27. **Safety Alignment Depth:** Evaluating safety boundaries.
28. **Llama 3:** Next generation open weights.
29. **DPO vs PPO:** Direct Preference Optimization analysis.
30. **MMAU Benchmark:** Multimodal agent evaluations.
31. **Scaling Laws for Precision:** Impact of quantization on scaling.
32. **Phi-4-Small:** High performance on edge devices.
33. **LLMs Think Too Fast:** Forcing intermediate reasoning steps.
34. **LLM Agents in Search:** Agentic web browsing strategies.
35. **CartesianMoE:** Advanced mixture of experts routing.
36. **Agent Evaluation Survey:** How to actually test agents.
37. **PaperBench:** Benchmarking research assistants.
